What is Samsung GSAP?

2018 Samsung global startup acceleration program is a part of Samsung employee volunteer program. Since 2010, about 1,500 employees have joined this program in 43 countries. Especially this year, Samsung focus on the local startup to support local entrepreneurs to build their own business and growth for sustainable impact in society. This program is being held globally in Indonesia, Malaysia, India and South Africa this year from July to September and about 80 selected startups are going to join this program.

South Africa GSAP

From the course of July to August, on-line mentoring is being conducted for the 20 startups from South Africa.  Local Boot Camp will be held from 8 to 13, September at Nangila located in Cape town. This program is going to be running for a week and at the end of the program, there will be a pitching event. Any participants who has business potential will have extended support from the local incubation center for the next 6 months.

Mentee benefits

1. Rewards for the top3 teams from South Africa (+ follow-up support)

2. Selected 5 teams from 4 countries will be invited to Samsung Head quarter in Korea.

3. Additional follow-up mentoring for the non-top3 teams (progress details on local incubation are currently in discussion)
